Journey


YP

Please introduce your journey so far from coming to Berlin.
In addition to your well-known experiences, what kind of background and shifts did you go through?
And how do you identify yourself as an artist?



1998–


Food experiments are the existing format because they last maybe ten years or so much longer but have become popular in the last six or seven years.
I have been practicing since maybe 1998 as an artist, but my background was oil painter, but I didn’t like tangible material, and I switched to the more event-based project, and I did something called pop-up café 1998 with 11 other artists. They had quite many different backgrounds.

pop-up café



pop-up café


pop-up café is a temporary site for dialogue that occasionally appears in different cities, each questioning a different set of boundaries. By identifying the context and parameters of the set topic, questioning its various aspects, and examining our positions, relations, and dynamics, we effectively cross and transform boundaries.

Asako Iwama, Seiji Ohba, Yoshiko Morita, Yayoi Yamashiro
https://www.asakoiwama.net/pop-up-cafe/
